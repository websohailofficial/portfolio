How I Work

In 6 Steps

Learn more about how I work with you from Introduction to launch your project, and beyond. I'll be with you every step of the way.

Discovery Call

01

We'll start with a free discovery call where I can earn more about your needs and wants. It's important for me to learn more about you and the goal you want to achieve.

Proposal & Contract

02

I'll send you a work plan based on what we discussed and a quote. I charge on a per project basis, so there'll be no suprises when comes to budget.

Content

03

We'll work to gather all the content needed like images,logo,Headlines etc. It is critical to have all the content ready in order to make the best possible design.

Design & Development

04

The fun begins! I'l keep you up to date throughout the whole design and development process. If at any point you have any questions, reach out me and I'll be happy to answer.

Project Delivery

05

Congrats, Your project is complete! But our relationship doesn't end there. I'll be answer any remaining questions and also provide any training/instructions that you may need.

Support

06

Websites are living breathing creatures. If problems do pop-up, everysit I build comes with 30 days of post launch support. If you need more support beyond that, I'm only a few clicks away.
